Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly do you respond?

We typically reply to all inquiries within 24 business hours. For urgent support regarding existing projects, please use the dedicated support line provided in your client dashboard.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Absolutely! We love helping new businesses get off the ground. We have specific "Launch Packages" designed to get startups up and running with a brand identity and MVP quickly.

What is your typical project timeline?

Timelines vary by project scope. A standard corporate website typically takes 4-6 weeks, while complex web applications or e-commerce platforms may take 3-6 months. We provide a detailed timeline during the proposal phase.

Do you offer post-launch maintenance?

Yes, we offer various ongoing support packages. These include server monitoring, security updates, content updates, and monthly performance reports to ensure your digital asset remains secure and effective.

What is your pricing structure?

We operate on both fixed-price and time-and-materials models, depending on the clarity of the project scope. For undefined scopes, we recommend an initial discovery phase to accurately estimate costs.
